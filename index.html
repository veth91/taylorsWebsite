About Taylor

At eight-years-old, I filled a notebook with a story about a young girl saving the world from a hoard of evil teachers with her sidekick named “Dude.” I’ve been trying to live up to that accomplishment ever since.

I graduated from the University of Montana with two degrees in Journalism and Environmental Studies (yes — I’m one of those Millennial overachievers). I’ve been published on Montana Public Radio, Montana PBS, The Missoulian, Outdoors Unlimited and others.

When I’m not revising my young-adult science fiction novel, I’m writing SEO blogs and consulting in digital marketing. In my free time, I like to take pictures, hike, camp, travel and pretend I can design as well as Paula Scher.
